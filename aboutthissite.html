About this site

The idea of making a website came to my mind a few days ago when I was wondering how should I wish you on **th of August. My initial stand was just a message "Happy birthday cute girl who turned into a mute girl" 😅 but then I thought, wait a minute if someone is on Read only mode then why not make your own platform where you have the freedom of speech. And here I am.

   Let me first tell you I had no idea how to make a website. Five days ago I did not know what is HTML, CSS, Javascript, ASP.Net MVC, JSON, web hosting, deployment etc etc. I mean I had heard these things but never knew the exact functioning of these in order to make an application. I know it's irrelevant to you but since I have to make content and to make content I have to say all these. Now I realise I am not a good content writer. I am not a good designer too.
On that note let me tell you the journey of this site. In today's era anything we (engineers) have to achieve starts with youtube videos. I also started watching all those html css javascript videos. It looked pretty simple that time but when I engaged myself started making it I realised it was not that straight forward.
          First problem : Designing a template
It had not been a while since I started making it that I stuck on designing the structure and moreover the colours. They really faded up my brain. Any colour I was choosing looked bad or the text would not be that clear in the background. It stil has that issue but I am done with it, whatever it is, it's final. Co-incidently it matched with the background image colours. Green leaves, your black dress and milky white face. Exactly these colours are used in the design.
Another issue:Database and deployment
I wanted a dynamic website where the server can interact with the client but that required a database but then deploying it was tricky. In case you don't know, deploying means publishing the codes that I have on my machine to a web hosting agency so that my website can be accessed to anyone. To understand the whole scenario of hosting and deployment took 2 days of workhour. Any video I was following resulted into some kind of failure. At last I found a combination of two to three videos to analyze the crucial steps and got successfull.
Not over yet: Device issue
The moment I thought I was ready to go, I found out that the website will open completely different on mobile and all of my designing was just a mess in the monbile view. I had to redesign for the mobile browser as well. Now when I code something with respect to mobile the desktop version would get hampered. This was real frustration. Finally I found a middle ground where both get descent looks and my website was ready for deployment. That moment was so pleasant knowing that my website can be accessed by anyone anywhere in the world.
Last but not the least: Content
Now the real goal of any service is what goal it achieves in my case it was to say you hbd. But a website requires a lot to be delivered so I thought of these content what you see on the page. But these are also some kind of burden when you have to wake up whole night typing some thoughts without having a thought.

Still what matters the most is the end result and I am very delighted to have it. I don't know if you will read this far but if you are here then I have something to say. No matter how you react to this website, I will always be thankful to you for being the reason for me to start making something productive.

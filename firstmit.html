First time I saw You

I had seen your name in freshers welcome notice about performance in dancing. I did not care that time. I didn't even see your performance but your name got noticed to me since you are titled "Shrivastava" and I also belong to kayastha family. For a moment I thought who is this girl. See I am not a castist but when you are a part of a system you inherit these properties of noticing someone from same community.
Few days later we were going to IIT Patna to play a cricket tournament and for a fraction of second I saw a pretty beautiful girl in our college through the bus window. After the game was over I asked my junior I saw a gorgeous girl in our college, had never seen earlier, do you know anyone new?(It was the first time I was taking interest in a girl) He said I don't know whom you saw, but I know one from first year. And you know what, he took your name.
I was pretty normal that time because I had this perception that engineers always exaggerate the ladies and also I thought whatever she is in first year so "apne ko kya matlab". After some days I again heard your name then I searched you on fb and my reaction was like "yeah pretty girl" and for the first time I sent friend request to a junior accepted it. But still there was a this senior junior doubt in my brain. Again after some days your name popped up and then I decided to find you out. Mid sem exam started and I found you out and saw you for the first time and the first words that came to my mind was "Omg the most beautiful girl I have seen ever..". Again outside the canteen I saw you and even you looked at me for a moment(maybe you don't remember but that moment was special for me) and I gazed into those deep calm eyes that changed me completely. Earlier I had never thought that in 3rd year(academically) I would be thinking about a girl. Because it was the time to work hard and make up the loss that I had faced earlier.

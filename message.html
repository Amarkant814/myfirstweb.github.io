Now a days there are tons of websites that we need to check in. In order to maintain the data they ask you to register on their websites. I can say that if there are even lakhs of websites that I register I will never forget the password of any website at any point of time in life. How ?
It's simple I use a technique where my password resides in the name the website itself and it's completely secure.

Suppose you need to register on makemytrip.com then the current password norm says it should be atleat of 8 character length, should consist of alphanumeric and a special character. So what we can do is that we can split the 8 length password into 5+3 in which the 5 characters will always be same following password norms. For example "A276@" and for remaining three you can choose the characters from the given site name placed on your favourite 3 digit number suppose your favourite 3 digit no is 591 the you choose the character at 5th position 9th position and 1st position as your reemaining password characters. In our case 5th character in "makemytrip" is m, 9th is i and 1st is m. so my last three characters are mim and thus our password is "A276@mim". Now what will be the password for swiggy. let's make it since we have selected the constant part as "A276@" we only need to find the last three characters on 591 rule. 5-'g' 9-since it doesnot have 9 characters put swiggy on loop and then find out 9th character you will find out 'w' comes at 9th position and 1st is 's' . so password="A276@gws"
The major drawback is that if a person saw your password and understands your trick he wil have access to all your accounts. So never let them know how are you selecting the password, what are your magical last three numbers and most imprtant of all never let the first five constant characters leak. By this way you don't need to remember any password. They are there. Tip: choose wisely your constants and last three.

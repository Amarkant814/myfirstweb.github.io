First year was going awesome but then something unusal happened and I was awarded a year back(in an unconventional manner i.e. I didn't fail..to know more goto pandemic section) and it changed my life style completely. I tried three ways to get out of college and get indulged in other streams. I gave NDA exam cleared written but in interview when the interviewer asked why do you want to join now you are well placed in nit patna. I had no satisfactory answer to that question. I was eliminated on the 5th day(Conference out).
